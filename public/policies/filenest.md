SCOPE

PRIVACY POLICY  


Effective Date: 05 May 2026  


We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of any significant  

changes within five (5) days of the update.  



COLLECTION OF DATA  


We may collect the following information through the App:  


1. Device Information: Non-personal data such as device model, Android version, signal  

strength, available storage, hardware specifications, and Device ID. This data is collected  

only when a user reports a bug.  


2. Regional Data: Your general location (limited to State/Country) via IP address for statistical purposes and policy enforcement.  


3. Third-Party Services: We utilize third-party services including Google Ads and Google Drive (if  

signed in). These services may collect data according to their own policies. You can review  

their privacy policies here.  


Data processed locally (Not sent to our servers):  


• Files selected for processing.  

• Device status indicators (e.g., Dark/Light mode, battery percentage).  



GOOGLE DRIVE:  


Access taken to your Google Drive If you sign up for direct G-Drive Uploads are:  


1. Full Access for Write and to Delete of a Specific Folder (Preferably Uniquator Filenest)  

created specifically for app and categorised sub-folder for specific type of file.  


We don’t take access to any other folder of your Google Drive.  




DATA PROTECTION  


While no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, we implement commercially  

reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.  



DATA STORAGE  


• Company Servers: Data collected by us is stored on secure servers.  


• Local Storage: Data that is not transmitted to our servers remains stored locally on your  

device.  


• Cloud Storage: Files backed up to Google Drive are stored on Google’s servers. Please  

review the Google’s Privacy Policy for more details.  



YOUR RIGHTS  


You have the following rights regarding your data:  


• Right to Erasure: You may request the deletion of data associated with your unique identifier  

(e.g., Device ID).  


• Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw your consent for data collection at any time.  

Please note that the App may cease to function upon withdrawal.  


Exceptions:  


• IPv4 or IPv6 addresses are not recognized as unique identities for the purpose of data  

deletion requests.  



CHILDREN’S PRIVACY  


We do not knowingly collect data from minors under the age of 13. If we discover that a minor has  

provided us with personal information, we will immediately cease collection and delete the associated  

data.  



USE OF YOUR DATA  


We utilize your data for the following purposes:  


1. Statistical analysis and reporting.  


2. Improving our services and user experience.  


3. Enforcement of our policies and terms.
