Toolkit Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

Thank you for using Toolkit. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Toolkit accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Collect

Toolkit is designed with privacy in mind.

  • We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data.
  • We do not upload your files, photos, documents, or app usage data to our servers.
  • Your data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

2. Permissions We Request

Nearby Devices

This permission allows Toolkit to discover and connect with nearby devices for the File Sharing feature.

Toolkit does not use this permission to track your location or identify nearby people.

Usage Access

This permission is required for the App Lock feature. It allows Toolkit to detect when a protected application is opened so that authentication can be displayed.

Toolkit does not collect, store, or transmit your app usage history.

Draw Over Other Apps

This permission allows Toolkit to display the App Lock screen over protected applications before they can be accessed.

It is used solely for the intended App Lock functionality.

3. Advertising

Toolkit may display advertisements using Google AdMob.

Google may collect certain information, such as advertising identifiers, to provide and personalize ads according to its own Privacy Policy. Toolkit does not control Google's data collection practices.

4. Data Storage

Toolkit stores settings and preferences locally on your device. We do not operate servers that collect or store your personal information.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)

These services have their own Privacy Policies and Terms of Service.

6. Children's Privacy

Toolkit is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the Last Updated date at the top of this page.
