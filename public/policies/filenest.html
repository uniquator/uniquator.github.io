PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: 05 May 2026

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of any significant changes within five (5) days of the update.

SCOPE

  • "We," "Our," and "Us" refer to Uniquator within this document.
  • "App" refers to Filenest within this document.

COLLECTION OF DATA

We may collect the following information through the App:

  • Device Information: Non-personal data such as device model, Android version, signal strength, available storage, hardware specifications, and Device ID. 📱
    • This data is collected only when a user reports a bug.
  • Regional Data: Your general location (limited to State/Country) via IP address for statistical purposes and policy enforcement.
  • Third-Party Services: We utilize third-party services including Google Ads and Google Drive (if signed in).
    • These services may collect data according to their own policies. You can check out Google's Policy here and Your data safety for Ads here.

Data processed locally (Not sent to our servers):

  • Files selected for processing.
  • Device status indicators (e.g., Dark/Light mode, battery percentage).

GOOGLE DRIVE

If you sign up for direct G-Drive Uploads, the following access is required:

  • Full Access for Write and to Delete of a Specific Folder (Preferably Uniquator Filenest) created specifically for the app and categorised sub-folders. ☁️
  • We do not take access to any other folder of your Google Drive.

DATA PROTECTION & STORAGE

While no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, we implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect your information.

  • Company Servers: Data collected by us is stored on secure servers.
  • Local Storage: Data not transmitted to our servers remains stored locally on your device.
  • Cloud Storage: Files backed up to Google Drive are stored on Google’s servers.

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Erasure: You may request the deletion of data associated with your unique identifier (e.g., Device ID). 🔐
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw your consent for data collection at any time.
    • Please note that the App may cease to function upon withdrawal.
  • Exceptions: IPv4 or IPv6 addresses are not recognized as unique identities for deletion requests.

CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

We do not knowingly collect data from minors under the age of 13. If we discover that a minor has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete the associated data.

USE OF YOUR DATA

We utilize your data for the following purposes:

  • Statistical analysis and reporting.
  • Improving our services and user experience.
  • Enforcement of our policies and terms.
